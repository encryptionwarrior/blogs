### Intro to soft skills
In IT Suport, you work with users to fix technology and improve how people use it. To accomplish this, you need to develop a trust between you and the user. 

- Exibitiing empathy
- Being conscious of your tone 
- Acknowledging the the person 
- Developing Trust 


- Empathy and acknowledgement are a big part of building trust. Without these, you'll find it difficult to connect with the user 

- Be honest with the user, even if you think they won't be happy about it.


### Anatomy of an Interaction 
Just be professional, ackknowledge the user, and show them some respect.
